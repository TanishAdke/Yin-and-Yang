Competition winning Portfolio concept made using Yin and Yang Philosophy.
Conept:
  The concept of Yin and Yang refers to:
  "In light, there's darkness, and in dark there's lightness"
This concept was utilized by me and a friend of mine in an national level event held at my college.

The concept and it's implementation in an portfolio:
Taking a web stack into mind, we get 3 major sides such as FrontEnd Development, BackEnd development and FullStack development, which can be categorized into:
Yin - Frontend Development, to learn the frontend, you must be a little bit knowledgeable about backend structure.
Yang - Backend Development, to learn the backend, you must be a little bit knowledgeable about frontend structure.
Yin and Yang - FullStack development, the utilization of both Yin and Yang is important, as such you get a complete structure.

Aside from the concept, the website was made using:
NextJS
TypeScript
